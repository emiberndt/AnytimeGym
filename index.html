About

Always open

Our facilities are always open for you, 24 hours a day, every day!

To succeed with your goals our philosophy is that opening hours should not stand in the way. That's why we want to give our members the opportunity to access our gym every hour of the day and night, all year around.

Latest equipment

Our gym has the latest technology and equipment in the industry!

We want to always provide our members with the best and latest within the industry. With the right tools you'll get the most out of your training, and will give you as a member the best conditions to succeed with your goals.

Experienced staff

Our staff always available to assist anytime of the day and night!

Personal trainers, instructors, as well as our passionated gym staff is always available to assist you. All of our staff is well educated and will provide you with all the help you'll need to get the most out of your workouts.
